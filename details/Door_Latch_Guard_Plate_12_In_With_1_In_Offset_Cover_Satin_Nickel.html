Enhance Your Home or Business Security with the Door Latch Guard Plate

This door latch guard plate is a reliable security upgrade designed to protect your doors from prying and forced entry. With its durable hardened stainless steel construction, this guard plate offers long-lasting strength and resistance against tampering.

Featuring a 1-inch offset cover, it ensures maximum protection by reducing the risk of damage to your door hardware. The L-shaped design fits perfectly around door latches, blocking access and deterring unwanted attempts to open your doors. Its satin nickel finish adds a modern, sleek look that complements your existing door hardware while delivering practical security benefits.

This guard plate is specifically crafted for outward-opening doors, providing an effective barrier against tampering. It works well with flush or slightly recessed doors and is ideal for double doors that do not have a center mullion. The 2.5mm thickness guarantees no deformation, making it a robust solution for both residential and commercial settings.

Install it easily using screws or utilize the included silicone pad for a secure, snug fit. Whether you're looking to reinforce your door's integrity or enhance its appearance, this door latch guard plate offers the perfect blend of style and substance.
