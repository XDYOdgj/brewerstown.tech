KES Matte Black Pop Up Drain Stopper

Elevate your bathroom's style and functionality with the KES Matte Black Pop Up Drain Stopper. This premium-quality drain stopper is built for long-lasting performance, combining the strength of all-metal construction with rustproof brass and 304 stainless steel.

Durable and Stylish Design

The matte black finish not only looks sophisticated but also resists scratches, corrosion, and tarnishing, ensuring the stopper remains in excellent condition for years to come. Its sleek appearance complements modern bathroom decor, making it a perfect addition to any vanity or countertop setup.

Effective Leak Prevention

Equipped with a 3-piece sealing ring design, this drain stopper is engineered to prevent water leaks and maintain optimal water flow. The included high-quality silicone gaskets provide added flexibility and durability, ensuring a secure fit and efficient operation.

Designed to fit vessel or undermount sinks, this drain stopper is ideal for those who want to avoid overflow. Its drain hole diameter of 1.6 to 1.9 inches ensures compatibility with a wide range of sink models, making installation quick and hassle-free.
