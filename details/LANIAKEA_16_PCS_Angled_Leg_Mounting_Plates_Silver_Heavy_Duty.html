Upgrade Your Furniture with 16pcs Angled Leg Mounting Plates

Made from premium quality steel with a durable zinc finish, these angled leg mounting plates are built to last. They provide excellent corrosion resistance and strong support for your furniture legs, making them perfect for reinforcing or replacing damaged frames. The 12-degree angle design ensures a stable base, giving your furniture a more modern and robust appearance. Whether you're restoring an old table or enhancing your living room set, these plates offer a secure and elegant solution.
